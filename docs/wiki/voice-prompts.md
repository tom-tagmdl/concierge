# Voice Prompts

These are practical prompts you can start using right away.

## Room and Location

- What room am I in?
- What do you call this?
- What can I do here?

## Lighting and Comfort

- Turn on the lights.
- Dim the lights in here.
- Close the shades.

## Media

- Start movie time.
- Pause the media.
- Resume what we were doing.
- Follow me music.
- Move music here.
- Bring music here.

## Calendar, Email, Tasks, Shopping

- What is on my calendar today?
- Summarize important email.
- What is left on my to-do list?
- What is on the shopping list?

## Household Updates

- What should I know?
- What happened while I was away?
- What still needs attention?

## Messaging

- Send a reminder to Alex.
- Let Jordan know dinner is ready.
- Notify me on mobile when this is done.

## Tips For Better Results

- Keep room names simple and consistent.
- Keep person names consistent with Home Assistant people.
- If a prompt gives the wrong result, update room aliases in setup.

## Follow-Me Notes

Follow-Me may refuse handoff when:

- identity confidence is low or unresolved
- room transition is ambiguous
- manual stop is active
- cooldown is active

See [Follow-Me Media](follow-me-media.md).
