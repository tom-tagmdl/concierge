# Email

This page helps you use email summaries in Concierge.

## What You Need First

1. Connect your email source to Home Assistant (or your chosen email summary integration).
2. In Concierge person setup, choose the correct mailbox for each person.

## What You Can Ask

- Summarize important email.
- What do I need to respond to today?
- Any urgent messages I should know about?

## Best Practice

- Set one primary mailbox per person.
- Keep mailbox names clear so they are easy to choose in setup.

## If It Is Not Working

- Confirm email integration is healthy in Home Assistant.
- Confirm the mailbox mapping in Concierge person setup.
- Retry with a simple prompt like Summarize important email.
