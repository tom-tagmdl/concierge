# General Information

Use these prompts when you want a quick household update.

## Questions To Ask

- What should I know?
- What happened while I was away?
- What still needs attention?
- Give me a household status update.

## How To Get Better Answers

- Keep room setup accurate.
- Keep person setup accurate.
- Keep calendar, email, and list integrations connected.

## Input vs Output (Important)

Concierge can hear a command from one device and respond on another device.

Example:

- You speak to the room voice assistant.
- Concierge can respond on that voice assistant, on the room speakers, on mobile, or in the UI, depending on your routing setup.

To control this behavior, review [Initial Room Setup](room-setup.md) and [Services Reference](services-reference.md).

## Good Follow-Up Prompts

- What can I do here?
- What should happen next?
- Remind me about this later.
