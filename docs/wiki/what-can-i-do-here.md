# What Can I Do Here

This is one of the most useful Concierge prompts.

Ask it when you want to know what Concierge can control in your current room.

## Prompts To Use

- What can I do here?
- What actions are available in this room?
- What can you control in this space?

## What You Should Expect

Concierge should list actions that match the room you are in.

## If The Answer Is Too Broad Or Wrong

- update room aliases
- update room device mappings
- check merged-room setup if you use merged rooms
- re-test the same prompt
