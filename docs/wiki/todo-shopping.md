# To-Do and Shopping Lists

This page helps you use task and shopping list features.

## What You Need First

1. Connect your task and shopping integrations to Home Assistant.
2. In Concierge person setup, choose the right task list and shopping list for each person.

## What You Can Ask

- What is left on my to-do list?
- What is on the shopping list?
- What still needs attention before tonight?

## Best Practice

- Keep list names simple.
- Make sure each person is linked to the right lists.

## If It Is Not Working

- Confirm task/shopping integrations work directly in Home Assistant.
- Confirm list mappings in Concierge person setup.
- Re-test with a short prompt.
