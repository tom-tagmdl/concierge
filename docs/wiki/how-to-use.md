# How To Use Concierge

This page is the day-to-day guide.

## A Simple Daily Pattern

1. Ask what is happening now.
2. Ask Concierge to do something in your room.
3. Ask for your schedule and message summaries.
4. Ask what still needs attention.

## Directing Where Responses Go

Concierge can hear you on one device and reply on another.

- In-room voice reply: use your room voice assistant path.
- Speaker reply: use your room speaker/media player path.
- Mobile or UI reply: use messaging and automation targets.

If routing feels wrong, confirm your room voice assistant and speaker selections in [Initial Room Setup](room-setup.md).

## Common Everyday Prompts

### Get a quick update

- What should I know?
- What happened while I was away?
- What still needs attention?

### Control your room

- Turn on the lights.
- Close the shades.
- Start movie time.

### Continue and Follow-Me media

- Continue music keeps playback governed by room media continuity context.
- Follow-Me requests a room-transition handoff when policy allows.

Try:

- Continue music.
- Follow me music.
- Move music here.

Important:

- Room-aware playback, merged-room playback, and Follow-Me playback are separate behaviors.
- Follow-Me is policy-governed and may refuse handoff when identity or transition authority is insufficient.

### Discover what is available

- What can I do here?
- What do you call this?

### Send reminders

- Send a reminder to Alex.
- Notify me on my phone when this is done.

For more examples, see [Voice Prompts](voice-prompts.md).

## If Responses Feel Wrong

1. Check room setup.
2. Check person setup.
3. Check calendar/email/task/shopping setup.
4. Use [Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md).
